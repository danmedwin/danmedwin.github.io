About

A rabbi who codes.
An educator who innovates.

I'm Rabbi Dan Medwin — an ordained Reform Rabbi with a Masters in Jewish Education, and a lifelong technologist. My career spans synagogue tech consulting, developing Visual T'filah, creating digital prayer books and apps with the CCAR, and co-directing a Jewish STEM summer camp.

Today I help Jewish organizations, schools, and communities navigate the intersection of tradition and technology — from AI literacy workshops for seniors to ethical tech curricula for teens, and everything in between.
